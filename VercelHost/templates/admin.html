Moderation Guidelines

Approve Images That:

  • • Show positive recovery moments
  • • Depict facility activities appropriately
  • • Maintain client privacy and dignity
  • • Are of good quality and relevant
  • • Have appropriate content for all audiences
  • • Support the center's mission and values

Reject Images That:

  • • Violate client privacy or confidentiality
  • • Show inappropriate or offensive content
  • • Are of poor quality or blurry
  • • Contain identifying information without consent
  • • Promote substance use or harmful activities
  • • Are unrelated to the center's mission

Privacy & Safety First

Always prioritize client privacy and safety. When in doubt, it's better to reject an image than to risk compromising someone's confidentiality or dignity.
