About Victory Rehabilitation Centre

Every Life Matters

Our Story

Victory Rehabilitation Centre was founded with a simple but powerful belief: Every Life Matters. Located along the Litein-Kericho Highway near Chelilis, we serve the South Rift region with compassionate, professional addiction treatment services.

Under the leadership of our Director, Robert Kerich, we have built a reputation for excellence in addiction treatment, combining evidence-based practices with a deeply personal approach to care.

Our team understands that addiction affects not just the individual, but entire families and communities. That's why we take a holistic approach to treatment, addressing the mind, body, and soul while supporting family involvement throughout the recovery process.
