Admissions Process

We've made our admissions process as simple and comfortable as possible. Our caring staff is here to guide you through every step of the way.

1. Initial Contact

Call us or visit our facility for a confidential consultation. We're available 24/7 to answer your questions and provide support.

2. Assessment

Our clinical team will conduct a comprehensive assessment to understand your specific needs and create a personalized treatment plan.

3. Admission

Once your treatment plan is ready, we'll help you with the admission process and ensure a smooth transition into our program.

What to Bring

Identification Documents

National ID, passport, or other valid identification

Medical Records

Any relevant medical history, current medications, and previous treatment records

Insurance Information

Insurance cards and payment method information

Personal Items

Comfortable clothing, toiletries, and personal items (see restrictions below)

Items Not Allowed

Alcohol & Substances

Any alcohol, illegal drugs, or non-prescribed medications

Electronic Devices

Mobile phones, tablets, or other electronic devices (secured during treatment)

Medications

All medications must be reviewed and approved by our medical team

Inappropriate Content

Magazines, books, or materials with inappropriate content
