Our Services

Comprehensive care for lasting recovery

Treatment Programs

At Victory Rehabilitation Centre, we offer a comprehensive range of evidence-based treatment programs designed to address the unique needs of each individual on their recovery journey.

Medical Detoxification

Safe, medically supervised detoxification process with 24/7 medical monitoring to ensure your safety and comfort during withdrawal.

  • • 24/7 medical supervision
  • • Medication-assisted treatment
  • • Withdrawal symptom management
  • • Nutritional support

Residential Treatment

Intensive residential program providing a structured, supportive environment for comprehensive addiction treatment and recovery.

  • • 24/7 supportive environment
  • • Structured daily programming
  • • Individual and group therapy
  • • Life skills training

Individual Counseling

One-on-one therapy sessions with licensed addiction counselors to address personal challenges and develop coping strategies.

  • • Personalized treatment plans
  • • Cognitive behavioral therapy
  • • Trauma-informed care
  • • Relapse prevention planning

Group Therapy

Peer support groups led by professional therapists to share experiences, build connections, and learn from others in recovery.

  • • Peer support networks
  • • Shared experiences
  • • Communication skills
  • • Accountability partnerships

Family Therapy

Family-focused treatment to heal relationships, improve communication, and build a strong support system for long-term recovery.

  • • Family education programs
  • • Communication improvement
  • • Relationship healing
  • • Support system building

Aftercare Support

Ongoing support and resources to help maintain sobriety and prevent relapse after completing primary treatment.

  • • Alumni programs
  • • Ongoing counseling
  • • Relapse prevention
  • • Community resources

Specialized Programs

Targeted treatment approaches for specific needs

Alcohol Addiction Treatment

Comprehensive alcohol addiction treatment addressing both physical dependence and psychological factors contributing to alcohol use disorder.

  • • Alcohol-specific detox protocols
  • • Behavioral modification therapy
  • • Liver function monitoring
  • • Nutritional rehabilitation

Substance Abuse Treatment

Evidence-based treatment for various substance addictions including prescription drugs, illegal substances, and other addictive substances.

  • • Substance-specific protocols
  • • Medication management
  • • Harm reduction strategies
  • • Dual diagnosis treatment

Mental Health Support

Integrated mental health services addressing co-occurring disorders such as depression, anxiety, PTSD, and other mental health conditions.

  • • Dual diagnosis treatment
  • • Psychiatric evaluation
  • • Medication management
  • • Trauma-informed therapy

Relapse Prevention

Comprehensive relapse prevention strategies and tools to help maintain long-term sobriety and handle triggers and challenges.

  • • Trigger identification
  • • Coping skills development
  • • Emergency action plans
  • • Ongoing support systems
